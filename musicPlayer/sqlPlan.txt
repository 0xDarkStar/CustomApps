 SQL Layout:

 Songs:
    ID | Title | Artist | Has subs | Length
ex:  1 | Money | The Drums | True | 234

 Playlist:
    ID | Name | Length | Number of songs
ex:  1 | Hold | 234 | 1

 Songs and playlists relations:
    Song ID | Playlist ID
ex:  1 | 1

 Subs:
    ID | Song ID | Language (en for English, fr for French, you know)
ex:  1 | 1 | en
